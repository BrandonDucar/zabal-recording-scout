How To Use It

  1. Load an archive: use the public ZABAL demo, paste a compatible JSON URL, or paste JSON directly.
  2. Pick a lane: choose receipts, music, video, dashboards, Farcaster, governance, or setup help.
  3. Open a session: jump straight to the recording, YouTube mirror, or transcript.
  4. Turn it into a build: use the session angle as a prompt for a July artifact.
  5. Share your direction: copy a short note when you want feedback from the ZABAL crew.
